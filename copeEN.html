Floating house

If Bangkok really sinks, the floating house is an innovative way out by building a lightweight house to serve as a raft when floodwaters buoy the home up.

Type of Floating house

  • Permanently-floating homes in lakes and other waterways.
  • Homes that float only when floodwaters swell but sit on the ground during the dry season.

Water Transportation

During natural calamities like flood and rains, when rail and road transport is disrupted, relief operations can be operated through water transport. So, if we need to live our life with water, travel by boat can possibly be the best way.
