PREVENTION

How to prevent the catastrophe to happen in 2050

Things you can start from yourself
and what the society can do

Reduce the use of
Air conditioners

Cold air is heating the world. Air conditioners consume large amounts of electricity, and the fluorocarbons they use as refrigerants deplete the ozone layer and contribute to global warming.

Reduce the use of
single-use plastics

As it does, sunlight and heat cause the plastic to release potent greenhouse gases, leading to an alarming feedback loop. As our climate changes, the planet gets hotter, the plastic breaks down into more methane and ethylene, increasing the rate of climate change and perpetuating the cycle.

Forest preservation

Combat climate change by preserving trees, planting trees, allocate space for the right agriculture could help with climate change.

Build reservoir

It is a structure built across a stream or river to hold water back. Dams can be used to store water, control flooding, and generate electricity.

Avoid building tower and skyscraper

Sheer weight of urban build-up on clay soil and uncontrolled underground water extraction can be caused land subsidence.

Build
sea wall

A seawall works by reflecting incident wave energy back into the sea, thus reducing the energy available to cause erosion.

If you and the society
keep ignoring practicing those things,
